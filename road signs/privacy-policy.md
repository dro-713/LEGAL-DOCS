Road Signs EE is developed by Evgeny Drobyshev, operating as **Base Hangar Works**

what information Road Signs EE collects, why, and what rights you have.

Questions about this policy: **basehangarworks@gmail.com**

## What information we collect

Road Signs EE is built to collect as little as possible.

**We do not:**
- Require or support user accounts or logins
- Use advertising networks or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs
- Collect your name, physical address, or any government ID
- Access your contacts, photos, location, camera, or microphone

**Stored only on your device, never sent to us:**
Your favorite signs, quiz history/scores, and app preferences (theme, language) live
locally on your device only. We have no access to this data and it is never
transmitted anywhere.

**Sent to our backend:**
- To determine which sign categories are free and to show our partner-school list, the
  app fetches current configuration from our server. This request carries no personal
  identifier — just a technical request for current data.
- If you enter a partner discount code, the code is sent to our server to check
  whether it's valid. We store the code used, a temporary session identifier, and a
  timestamp — nothing that identifies you personally.
- If you tap a partner school's email, phone, or website link, we log an anonymous
  count of that action (which school, which action) so we can tell whether the
  partnership is being used. This is never tied to you individually and includes no
  device or user identifier.

**Purchases:**
If you unlock full access, the purchase is handled entirely by **Google Play
Billing**. We never see or store your payment details — Google processes that
directly under its own [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We only
learn that a purchase happened, never any financial detail.

**Technical/network data:**
Like any networked app, our backend (hosted on Google's Apps Script platform) will
see your device's IP address as an ordinary, unavoidable part of how any web request
works. We don't separately log, store, or use this to identify you.

## Why we process this information

- To let you redeem partner discount codes and unlock full access
- To show you accurate, current category and partner information
- To understand, in aggregate only, whether our partner program is actually being
  used — never at an individual level

## Who we share information with

- **Google** — for backend hosting (Google Sheets/Apps Script) and payment processing
  (Google Play Billing). Data passing through Google's infrastructure is governed by
  [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- We do not sell, rent, or share information with advertisers, data brokers, or anyone
  else.

## Data retention

- Redemption-code checks and anonymous tap counts are currently kept indefinitely for
  partnership tracking. Since none of it identifies you, there's no practical way for
  us to isolate a specific "your" entry — but you're welcome to contact us with any
  concern.
- Data stored on your device (favorites, quiz history, preferences) stays until you
  uninstall the app or clear its data via your device settings — entirely your call.

## Children's privacy

Road Signs EE is built for people learning to drive — generally teenagers and adults —
and isn't directed at young children. We don't knowingly collect information from
children.

## Your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection
Regulation, including to:

- Access information we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Data portability
- Lodge a complaint with your national authority — in Estonia, the
  [Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate](https://www.aki.ee/en)

Given how little personal information Road Signs EE actually collects, most of these
will have limited practical scope here — but reach out to
**basehangarworks@gmail.com** with any request.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. If we make a material change, the app
will prompt you to review and accept the updated policy the next time you open it —
we won't change terms on you silently.
